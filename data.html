Survey respondents, intruments used, and documentation

Consent Form Summary


All participants were informed of the study's purpose, procedures, and their rights as respondents. Written informed consent was obtained prior to data collection. Participants were assured of confidentiality and anonymity throughout the study.

×

Respondents Profile


The study involved 20 third-year Bachelor of Science in Biology (BSBIO) students as respondents. The survey was conducted online through Google Forms to evaluate the usability of the prototype.

SURVEY METHODOLOGY

Inruments Used

Two evaluation methods were used to assess the usability and effectiveness of the CareSync prototype.


Heuristic Evaluation Workbook

Each group member conducted an individual heuristic evaluation using the workbook to identify usability issues based on standard design principles.

Usability Testing Survey

Respondents answered an online usability survey to evaluate the prototype’s ease of use, functionality, and overall user experience.
